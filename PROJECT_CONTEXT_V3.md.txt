# Mandatory product rules — do not regress

## 1. Day rhythm is mandatory

Every generated day should follow:

```text
breakfast → morning activities → lunch → afternoon activities → dinner → nightlife
```

Do not order days by stale `sort_order` or raw clock time alone if that violates phase rhythm. Stops must be sorted and chained by phase.

Relevant file:

* `lib/itinerary/day-rhythm.ts`

## 2. Reservation anchors are sacred

User reservations must never be shifted by travel chaining.

Examples:

* Casa Batlló at 8:30 AM stays 8:30 AM.
* Icebarcelona at 8:45 PM stays 8:45 PM.
* Reserved restaurants should keep their reservation time and receive the correct `meal_type`.

Do not let `recomputeScheduleTimes` or `rescheduleStopsFromOrder` move anchors.

## 3. Meals must respect opening hours

Meals cannot be scheduled before the restaurant opens.

Important breakfast rule:

* Breakfast default floor is 9:00 AM when opening hours are unknown.

Relevant functions/files:

* `getMealEarliestMinutes`
* `resolveMealArrivalMinutes`
* `lib/itinerary/hours.ts`

## 4. Breakfast can become brunch after a morning anchor

If a morning reservation makes breakfast impossible, do not force breakfast before it.

Example:

* Casa Batlló at 8:30 AM.
* If no early-open restaurant fits before it, schedule brunch after the visit, around 10:15 AM or later.
* But do not schedule “breakfast” after lunch.

Relevant files:

* `lib/itinerary/meal-slots.ts`
* `lib/itinerary/smart-generate.ts`
* `lib/itinerary/day-rhythm.ts`

## 5. Three meals every day, but only one per meal type

Every trip day must have:

* breakfast
* lunch
* dinner

But there should only be one stop per `meal_type`.

Do not create:

* duplicate breakfasts
* duplicate lunches
* duplicate dinners
* breakfast after lunch
* breakfast/lunch at the same timestamp
* multiple back-to-back meal stops with no activity between, unless unavoidable due to reservations

Relevant files:

* `lib/itinerary/ensure-meals.ts`
* `lib/itinerary/meal-slots.ts`

## 6. Reserved restaurants need meal labels

If a reserved restaurant falls into a meal window, it should get `meal_type`.

Example:

* Cañete at noon should have `meal_type: lunch`.
* Antigua at 5 PM should count as dinner.

UI badge rule:

* Show meal badge when `meal_type` is set.
* Do not require `stop_type === "meal"`.

Relevant files:

* `lib/itinerary/hours.ts`
* `lib/itinerary/meal-locations.ts`
* `components/trip/itinerary-section.tsx`

## 7. Manual places are mandatory

Every `source = 'manual'` place must appear exactly once per trip.

Manual places are not optional suggestions. They must not be dropped because a suggested place has the same `google_place_id`.

If duplicate Google IDs exist:

* dedupe trip-wide by `google_place_id`
* prefer manual over suggested
* evict suggested duplicate if needed

Relevant files:

* `lib/itinerary/manual-places.ts`
* `lib/itinerary/smart-generate.ts`
* `lib/itinerary/suggestion-exclusions.ts`
* `lib/itinerary/day-place-usage.ts`

## 8. Suggested places stay out of My Places

`source = 'suggested'` places are for generation/Discover pools.

They should not appear in My Places unless the user manually saves them.

Map rules:

* My Map = manual places only.
* Plan map = itinerary stops only.
* Plan map can filter by selected itinerary day.

## 9. Two-pass scheduling must stay consistent

This has caused repeated regressions.

There are two timing passes:

```text
Placement pass:
addMeal, scheduleManualPlacesForDay, addBreakfastAfterMorningAnchor, ensureTripMeals, fillSparseDaysForTrip

Recompute/reschedule pass:
finalizeDayTimes → recomputeScheduleTimes
rescheduleStopsFromOrder
```

If a fix only updates placement logic, a later recompute can overwrite it.

Any fix to meal timing, opening hours, anchors, rhythm order, meal-slot bounds, duplicate meal cleanup, or visit caps must also be reflected in:

* `recomputeScheduleTimes`
* `rescheduleStopsFromOrder`

Relevant files:

* `lib/itinerary/schedule-times.ts`
* `lib/itinerary/reschedule-day.ts`

## 10. UX loading rules

Full-screen loading overlay only on Generate.

Do not hide the whole itinerary during:

* drag-and-drop
* directions fetch
* small reschedules
* stop edits

Drag should use a per-day spinner. Directions fetch should be non-blocking.

Relevant file:

* `components/trip/itinerary-section.tsx`

# Latest key learnings and decisions

## 1. Exclude list was poisoning Google search

Major root cause:

* 333 stale suggested place rows were all excluded from Google search.
* This blocked meal/activity insertion and caused empty or sparse days.

Fix:

* `getSuggestionExcludeGoogleIds` now excludes only:

  * manual places
  * current itinerary stops
* It should not exclude every suggested row in the DB.
* `ensure-meals.ts` uses the narrowed exclude list.
* `smart-generate.ts` uses `savedGoogleIds = manual only`.

Relevant file:

* `lib/itinerary/suggestion-exclusions.ts`

## 2. Trip-wide restaurant brand dedupe is required

Problem:

* “Billy Brunch,” “Billy Brunch Headquarters,” and “Billy Brunch & Park” had different Google IDs and kept repeating.

Fix:

* Added `meal-dedup.ts`.
* `restaurantBrandKey()` uses a brand-level key, roughly first 2 words.
* Used in:

  * Google meal fetch
  * `ensure-meals.ts`
  * `fill-sparse.ts`
  * `smart-generate.ts`

Relevant file:

* `lib/itinerary/meal-dedup.ts`

## 3. Late brunch needed special rhythm handling

Problem:

* `meal_type: breakfast` always sorted as phase 0.
* Brunch after Casa Batlló appeared before the anchor in the UI.

Fix:

* `day-rhythm.ts` treats breakfast after 10 AM but before lunch as MORNING phase.
* Added timing logic like `minutesAfterPriorMorningAnchors()`.

Relevant file:

* `lib/itinerary/day-rhythm.ts`

## 4. Anchor/overlap timing had to respect meal floors

Problem:

* `resolveMealArrivalMinutes` had an anchor-cap branch that forced impossible 8 AM breakfast before the 9 AM floor.

Fix:

* `hours.ts`, `schedule-times.ts`, and `reschedule-day.ts` now handle:

  * brunch after morning anchor
  * `getMealEarliestMinutes`
  * travel chaining where arrival must be at least `readyToLeave + travel`

Relevant files:

* `lib/itinerary/hours.ts`
* `lib/itinerary/schedule-times.ts`
* `lib/itinerary/reschedule-day.ts`

## 5. Reserved restaurant meal slots need better inference

Problem:

* Antigua at 5 PM did not count as dinner.
* System added a fourth restaurant unnecessarily.

Fix:

* `inferMealTypeFromMinutes` treats 4 PM+ as dinner.
* `dayMissingMeals` uses `reservation_time`.

Relevant files:

* `lib/itinerary/hours.ts`
* `lib/itinerary/meal-locations.ts`

## 6. Meal-slot enforcement is the latest critical fix

Problem examples:

* Jul 2: 12:30 PM “breakfast” after 12:00 PM lunch.
* Jul 4: breakfast and lunch both at 1:55 PM.
* Jul 5: meals back-to-back with no activity between.

Fix:

* New `meal-slots.ts`.
* Enforces one slot per meal type.
* Enforces insertion windows.
* Skips afternoon breakfast on excursion days.
* Dedupes invalid meals.
* Skips breakfast if it cannot end before lunch.
* Prevents same-time breakfast/lunch collisions.
* Helps require sightseeing between breakfast and lunch where expected.

Relevant file:

* `lib/itinerary/meal-slots.ts`

## 7. Sparse days need sightseeing minimums, not just stop count

Problem:

* Jul 11 could have enough total stops but still be all restaurants.
* `dayIsSparse` only counted total stops.

Fix:

* `fill-sparse.ts` now treats days as needing fill if `countSightseeingStops < 2`.

Relevant file:

* `lib/itinerary/fill-sparse.ts`
